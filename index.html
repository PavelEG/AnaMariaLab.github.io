Welcome to the lab!!

Research interests

  1. Biotechnologies for generating polyploids in mollusks for productive and mitigation purposes.
  2. Genomics of Nodipecten subnodosus associated with reproductive arrest and muscle growth in triploids.
  3. Genomics and genetics of the reproductive capacity of white shrimp Litopenaeus vannamei: development of transcriptomic tools.
  4. Selective breeding of the Pacific oyster Crassostrea gigas and development of transcriptomic tools for selection assisted by molecular markers.
